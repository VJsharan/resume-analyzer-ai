Save this file — this becomes your template. 

👉 Tip: Keep variable names short and descriptive (e.g., {{ project_summary }}). 

📤 3. Upload Your Template
On your Dashboard, click Create Template.

The platform will scan your file and automatically detect all variables ({{ … }}) you've added. 

✍️ 4. Define AI Prompts for Variables
After upload, you’ll see a list of all detected variables. For each:

Write an AI prompt — this tells the AI how to fill that section.

Example:

Variable: {{ executive_summary }}

Prompt: “Generate a 4–6 sentence business executive summary focused on project goals, key results, and next steps.” 

👉 Clear prompts = better AI output.
